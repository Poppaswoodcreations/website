About Poppa's Wooden Creations

Poppa's Wooden Creations began in 2015 when our founder, a passionate craftsman and grandfather, started making wooden toys for his grandchildren in his Whangarei workshop.

Today, we're proud to be one of New Zealand's premier wooden toy makers, creating heirloom-quality toys that inspire imagination, encourage learning, and provide endless hours of screen-free fun.

Our workshop in Whangarei is where the magic happens. Using traditional woodworking techniques combined with modern safety standards, we create toys that are not just beautiful, but built to last for generations.
